Jesus Heals the Ten Lepers

Jisas i mekim orait Tenpela Man i gat Sik Lepra

This story is from Luke chapter 17 verses 11-18.

Dispela stori i stap long buk bilong Luk sapta 17 ves 11 i go long 18.

Jesus and his disciples were on the way to Jerusalem and had reached the border between Galilee and Samaria.

Jisas, na ol disaipel bilong em, i bihainim rot i go long Jerusalem, na ol i wokabaut i go namel long distrik Samaria na Galili.

As Jesus entered a village, ten men with leprosy stood at a distance, crying out, ‘Jesus, Master, have mercy on us!’

Taim em i kamap long wanpela ples, ol tenpela sik lepra man i kam bungim em long rot, ol i sanap longwe, na ol i singaut, “Jisas, Bikman, yu ken sore long mipela?”

Jesus looked at them and said, ‘Go and show yourselves to the priests.’*

As the ten men obeyed, and continued down the road, they were cleansed of their leprosy.

Jisas i lukim ol, na em i tokim ol olsem, “Yupela go soim skin bilong yupela long ol pris.” Orait ol i harim tok na ol i wokabaut i go, na skin bilong ol i kamap klin.

*The local priest was the person who examined people, to see whether they had leprosy or not.

*Wanpela wok bilong pris - ol mas lukluk long wanwan man sapos em i gat sik lepa, o nogat.

One of them was a Samaritan man.  When he saw that he was healed, he came back to Jesus, shouting, ‘Praise God!’ He fell to the ground at Jesus’ feet, thanking him for what he had done.

Na wanpela bilong ol i man bilong Samaria, na taim em i lukim skin bilong en i gutpela gen, na em i go bek long Jisas na em i singaut bik maus na litimapim nem bilong God. Taim em i kamap long Jisas em i brukim skru na putim pes i go daun long graun klostu long lek bilong Jisas, na em i givim tenkyu long em long wanem samting em mekim long en.

Jesus asked, ‘Didn’t I heal ten men? Where are the other nine? Has no one else returned to give glory to God, except this foreigner?’

Jisas i lukim dispela man, na em i tok, “Mi ting tenpela man i kamap klin. Tasol ol nainpela man i stap we? Ating i no gat man bilong i kam bek na litimapim nem bilong God, a? Dispela man bilong narapela lain, em wanpela tasol.”

Jesus then said to the man, ‘Stand up and go. Your faith has healed you.’​

Na Jisas i tokim dispela man olsem, “Yu kirap na yu go. Bilip bilong yu i bin mekim yu i orait gen.”
