English
Animal Stories

This is Cat.

Dispela em Pusi.

This is Dog.

Dispela em Dok.

Cat and Dog each have a hat.

The hats are new. The hats are green.

Pusi na Dok i gat wan wan hat. Dispela tupela hat i nupela. Dispela tupela hat i grinpela.

Cat puts her hat on her head.

Pusi i putim hat bilong em antap long het bilong em.

Dog puts his hat on his head.

Dok i putim hat bilong em antap long het bilong em.

Cat and Dog go out for a walk. Cat and Dog walk to the water.

They walk to the water with their hats on their heads.

Pusi na Dok i wokabaut i go. Tupela i wokabaut i go long wara. Tupela i wokabaut i go long wara wantaim ol hats bilong ol.

Cat and Dog sit down at the water.

Cat and Dog look at the water.

Cat and Dog look at the water with their hats on their heads.

Pusi na Dok i sindaun arere long wara. Tupela i lukluk long wara. Tupela i lukluk long wara wantaim ol hats bilong ol.

Then the wind comes.

The wind blows

very hard.

Oh no!

The wind blows the hats

into the water!

Na win i kirap. Win i ron strong tru.

Olaman!

Win i rausim ol hat na karim i go insait long wara.

Cat and Dog cry.

Then Elephant comes by.

Pusi na Dok i krai. Na Elefan i wokabaut i kam.

Elephant hears Cat and Dog cry.

Elephant sees two hats in the water.

Then Elephant walks to the water. Elephant drinks the water.

Elefan i harim Pusi na Dok i krai. Em i lukim ol hats bilong tupela i stap insait long wara. Elefan i kam klostu long wara.  Elefan i stat long dringim olgeta wara long raunwara.

 He drinks and drinks and drinks.

Elephant drinks all the water!

Em i dring, na dring, na dring.

Elefan i dringim olgeta wara i go, na ol wara i pinis olgeta!

Now Cat and Dog can get their hats.

Nau Pusi na Dok inap kisim ol hat bilong tupela.

Cat puts her hat on her head. Dog puts his hat on his head.

Cat and Dog are happy. They smile.

Pusi i putim hat bilong em antap long het bilong em. Dok i putim hat bilong em antap long het bilong em. Tupela i amamas. Tupela i smail.

And Elephant...

Elephant has to go to the toilet!

Na Elefan . . .

Elefan i mas go rausim wara nau!

Bye-bye Cat. Bye-bye Dog. Bye-bye Elephant.

Tata Pusi. Tata Dok. Tata Elefan.
